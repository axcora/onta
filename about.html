About ONTA - The Philosophy of Speed

Reimagining the Static Web

Our mission is to provide a high-performance web architecture that is easy to deploy on any cPanel environment. We believe in the simplicity of static files and the raw power of established technologies.

The Axcora Zetta Core Vision

ONTA is more than just a tool; it represents a shift back to efficiency. As an architectural brand under Axcora, Zetta Core focuses on speed and precision. We bridge the gap between JAMSTACK technology and seamless AI integration.

Why Perl?

While the modern web is cluttered with heavy dependencies, we returned to Perl—the legendary king of text manipulation. This allows ONTA to achieve:
Surgical Precision: Processing content with minimal overhead.
Zero-Dependency Reliability: No more broken builds due to package updates.
Automated Ease: A robotic design concept that simplifies customer automation and API development.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to building a static site generator that empowers developers and business owners alike. By removing the complexity of modern deployment, we ensure your focus remains on what matters most: your content and your speed.
