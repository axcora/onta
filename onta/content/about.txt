## Reimagining the Static Web

Our mission is to provide a [high-performance web architecture]({{current_page}}) that is easy to deploy on any cPanel environment. We believe in the simplicity of static files and the raw power of established technologies.

### The Axcora Zetta Core Vision

ONTA is more than just a tool; it represents a shift back to efficiency. As an architectural brand under **Axcora**, Zetta Core focuses on speed and precision. We bridge the gap between [JAMSTACK technology]({{current_page}}) and seamless AI integration.


### Why Perl?

While the modern web is cluttered with heavy dependencies, we returned to **Perl**—the legendary king of text manipulation. This allows ONTA to achieve:

* **Surgical Precision:** Processing content with minimal overhead.
* **Zero-Dependency Reliability:** No more broken builds due to package updates.
* **Automated Ease:** A robotic design concept that simplifies [customer automation]({{current_page}}) and API development.

### Our Commitment

We are dedicated to building a [static site generator]({{current_page}}) that empowers developers and business owners alike. By removing the complexity of modern deployment, we ensure your focus remains on what matters most: your content and your speed.

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication. ONTA is the embodiment of that principle in web architecture." — **Axcora Zetta Core**
