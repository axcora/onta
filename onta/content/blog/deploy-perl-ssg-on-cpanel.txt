One of the biggest hurdles for modern SSG users is complex deployment pipelines. Many frameworks require specialized cloud runners or expensive edge computing. **ONTA SSG** breaks this barrier by being intentionally [cPanel-friendly]({{current_page}}).

### Why Choose cPanel for Static Sites?

Many developers think they need complex CI/CD for static sites. However, ONTA brings [professional static architecture]({{current_page}}) to the most accessible hosting platforms available. Since it generates pure HTML, CSS, and images, your site remains lightning-fast and ultra-secure.



### Step-by-Step Deployment

1.  **Generate the Build:** Run your ONTA engine to process your content files.
2.  **Surgical Precision:** ONTA uses [surgical text processing]({{current_page}}) to ensure your final assets are clean and ready for production.
3.  **Upload:** Simply upload the `build` or public folder via FTP or the cPanel File Manager.
4.  **Live:** Your site is now live on a [high-performance web architecture]({{current_page}}) without the need for server-side processing.

### The Power of Zero Dependencies

Because ONTA is built with the **Axcora Zetta Core** philosophy, it doesn't require complex Node.js or Python environments on your server. It is the perfect solution for users who want [high-performance web architecture]({{current_page}}) combined with the simplicity of traditional hosting.

> "Static sites shouldn't be hard to deploy. We brought Perl back to ensure your deployment is as fast as your site's load speed." — **Axcora Zetta Core**
