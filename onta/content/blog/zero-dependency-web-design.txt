Bloat is the enemy of the modern web. Most websites today carry megabytes of unnecessary JavaScript just to display simple text. With **ONTA SSG**, we return to the fundamentals of [high-performance web architecture]({{current_page}}).

### The Zetta CSS Philosophy

Our **Zetta CSS Engine** is built with pure, clean code—ensuring zero dependencies. By utilizing advanced CSS techniques, we provide responsive navigation and grids without requiring a single line of JavaScript. This approach ensures that your website remains lightweight, accessible, and incredibly fast on any mobile device.



### Why Zero Dependency Matters

In the world of [professional static architecture]({{current_page}}), every kilobyte counts. By removing heavy frameworks, we achieve:

* **Instant Load Times:** Reducing the Time to Interactive (TTI) to almost zero.
* **Maximum Accessibility:** Ensuring the site works perfectly even on slow networks or older browsers.
* **Surgical Precision:** Every style is intentional, leading to [automated ease of use]({{current_page}}) and easier maintenance.

### Clean Code, Powerful Results

As part of the **Axcora Zetta Core** brand, we advocate for [surgical text processing]({{current_page}}) and lean design. Our robotics-inspired concepts mean that automation should simplify, not complicate. When you strip away the bloat, you are left with the pure essence of your content.

> "The most powerful code is the code you didn't have to write. Minimalism isn't just an aesthetic; it's a performance strategy." — **Axcora Zetta Core**
