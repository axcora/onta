Deploying Perl SSG on Shared Hosting and cPanel

One of the biggest hurdles for modern SSG users is complex deployment pipelines. Many frameworks require specialized cloud runners or expensive edge computing. ONTA SSG breaks this barrier by being intentionally cPanel-friendly.

Why Choose cPanel for Static Sites?

Many developers think they need complex CI/CD for static sites. However, ONTA brings professional static architecture to the most accessible hosting platforms available. Since it generates pure HTML, CSS, and images, your site remains lightning-fast and ultra-secure.

Step-by-Step Deployment

Generate the Build: Run your ONTA engine to process your content files.
Surgical Precision: ONTA uses surgical text processing to ensure your final assets are clean and ready for production.
Upload: Simply upload the build or public folder via FTP or the cPanel File Manager.
Live: Your site is now live on a high-performance web architecture without the need for server-side processing.

The Power of Zero Dependencies

Because ONTA is built with the Axcora Zetta Core philosophy, it doesn't require complex Node.js or Python environments on your server. It is the perfect solution for users who want high-performance web architecture combined with the simplicity of traditional hosting.
"Static sites shouldn't be hard to deploy. We brought Perl back to ensure your deployment is as fast as your site's load speed." — Axcora Zetta Core
By moving to a static environment, you eliminate SQL injection risks and drastically reduce server load. Explore more automated ease of use techniques at our lab: www.axcora.my.id.
